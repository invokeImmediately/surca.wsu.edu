Application Guidelines

SURCA student participants,
 
Sharing or disseminating the results of your mentored research, scholarship, and creative activity publicly at the Showcase for Undergraduate Research and Creative Activities (SURCA) is an important aspect of being an undergraduate researcher.
 
Whether your work has just begun or you have completed your project with the guidance of a mentor, the university community is eager to see and hear about your results. A wide variety of student work is represented at SURCA, done by men and women from disciplines and majors all across the institution.
 
Congratulations on being selected to participant in SURCA and being an important member of WSU's vast research community. With your contributions, we truly are a world-class university.

—Shelley Pressley, Ph.D.

Director, WSU Office of Under-graduate Research

2012 SURCA award winners in the Engineering and Physical Sciences Category

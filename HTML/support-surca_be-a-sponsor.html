Be a Sponsor of SURCA

We are glad you would like to sponsor SURCA. Students who present their undergraduate research and creative activities at SURCA find it to be a transformative experience. The event could not occur without the support of individuals and corporations. More information about SURCA sponsorship can be found in our brochure, “Support for Undergraduate Researchers and the Future,” or by contacting one of the individuals listed below.

Ready to give now? You can give to SURCA using the WSU Foundation's online giving site.

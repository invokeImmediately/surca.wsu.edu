About SURCA Sponsorship

We are very pleased and honored that you are interested in helping to sponsor SURCA. Students who present their undergraduate research and creative activities at SURCA find it to be a transformative experience. All presenters can proudly add SURCA to their resume. And, many presenters will be judged “the best” in their category and receive an award..

The event, and SURCA awards, would not be possible without the support of generous individuals and corporations. For more information about SURCA sponsorship, please contact one of the individuals below. They can also explain details shown in our brochure, “Supporting Undergraduate Researchers: The Future of Our State, Nation, and World.”

Ready to give now? You can give to SURCA using the WSU Foundation’s online giving site.

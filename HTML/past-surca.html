Past SURCA Events

Showcase for Undergraduate Research and Creative Activities (SURCA) has been held annually since 2012. Being an evolution of the former Undergraduate Research Symposium, it is held in conjunction with Academic Showcase and was created to provide a single venue in which undergraduates can share their mentored research, scholarship, and creative activites that make an original intellectual or creative contribution to their chosen discipline. Since 2012, a total of 489 undergraduate students have presented their research and creative activities in SURCA, and 119 students have been awarded for their work.

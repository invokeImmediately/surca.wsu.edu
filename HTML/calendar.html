Information About SURCA: Calendar for SURCA 2019

Prior to SURCA

Check this section periodically for updates.

  • Talk to your mentor about presenting at SURCA, making a poster, submitting your online application including an abstract describing your work, and so on. Remember that applications are due online by Feb. 22.
  • Attend a poster-making workshop.
  • Design your poster and have it printed well in advance of the March 25 event.

Time Description Location

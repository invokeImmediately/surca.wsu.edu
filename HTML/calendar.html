Information About SURCA: Calendar for SURCA 2020

Prior to SURCA

Check this section periodically for updates.

  • Talk to your mentor about presenting at SURCA, making a poster, submitting your online application including an abstract describing your work, and so on. Remember that applications are due online by 11:59 p.m. on Feb. 25.
  • Attend a poster-making workshop.
  • Design your poster and have it printed well in advance of the March 25 event.

Free Poster Design Workshops

Dates: Friday, March 8 and Monday, March 18

Time: Drop by any time between 4:30 and 6:00 p.m.

Hosted By: Biomedical Communications Unit (BCU)

Location: Student lounge of Bustad Hall (just down the hall from the BCU office)

Stop by and see examples of good poster design as well as some things to avoid. You will get advice and tips and tricks to help you design your poster. Be sure to pick up a discount printing coupon while you’re there! Visit the BCU website to learn more.

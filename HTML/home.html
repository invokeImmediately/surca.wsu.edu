SURCA 2021 Will Be Virtual

To protect our undergraduate student participants, judges, and guests from COVID-19 transmission, SURCA 2021 will be virtual. Students will use the Zoom platform to share their work with the world and receive valuable feedback from experts.

New for SURCA This Year

  • There will be no posters this year. Instead, students will deliver their work virtually using slides made in PowerPoint or similar presentation software.
  • New “Research Proposal” presentation category. We have created a research proposal presentation category for students who have a mentored research underway but have not yet collected results they can share. This category could be especially applicable to our students whose work has been impacted by the conditions imposed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

About SURCA

SURCA is the only WSU-wide event featuring the faculty-mentored research, scholarship, and creative activities of undergraduates from all majors, grades, and campuses—work that makes an original intellectual or creative contribution to a discipline. It is a symposium hosted by the Office of Undergraduate Research, which is part of WSU Division of Academic Engagement and Student Achievement. SURCA is part of the annual WSU Showcase, highlighting academic achievements across WSU.

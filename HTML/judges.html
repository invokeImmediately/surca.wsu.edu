SURCA Judges

Information for Judges at SURCA

A draft copy of the SURCA 2016 Abstract Booklet will be available for judges to familiarize themselves with student abstracts ahead of time.

Judges are needed in all categories at SURCA, March 28, 2016, CUB Senior Ballroom.

An online form will be available to sign up to judge.

Who can judge at SURCA?

  • Faculty and post-docs from a post-secondary educational institution
  • Anyone with expertise and work experience from industry, government, non-profit organizations, and other entities outside of WSU

What if I am from off campus?

We are delighted to have you as a judge and we will do everything possible to make your visit to WSU an enjoyable one. We will provide complimentary parking. Contact UGresearch@wsu.edu for information.

How Much Time Will Judging Take?

  • Before the Event - You may want to review abstracts on the website.
  • 2:00-3:00 p.m. Optional time to review posters when students are not present. You may use none, some, or all of this time.
  • 3:00-4:00 p.m. Formal judging, students are present for questions.
  • 4:00 p.m. Scores must be turned in.
  • 4:00-4:45 p.m. Optional leisure time to look at posters and talk to students.
  • 4:45-5:30 p.m. You are invited to attend the award ceremony (optional).

Will I Be Judging Posters in My Own Field?

You select your preference for the categories that you wish to judge.

How Judging Works

  • You will be assigned 2 – 4 posters to judge.
  • You will be provided with a judging rubric and a score card.
  • You will also be given the opportunity to provide written feedback to students, as you find appropriate.
  • Judges will have their own area in the CUB Senior Ballroom where you can sit at a table, work on your score card, and enjoy refreshments reserved specifically for judges.
